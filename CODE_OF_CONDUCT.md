# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the TrustDB community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, education, appearance, nationality, language, or personal characteristics.

We will act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Assume good intent while asking for evidence where correctness or security matters.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's credentials, keys, customer data, or private correspondence.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about scope, safety, release quality, and community moderation.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, discriminatory language, or sexualized attention.
- Trolling, repeated disruption, or knowingly misleading technical claims.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors through coordinated spam or unsolicited promotion.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security contact when public reporting would expose personal or sensitive information:

https://github.com/wowtrust/trustdb/security/advisories/new

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is inconsistent with this code. Reports will be handled as privately as practical.
